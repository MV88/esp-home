Welcome to the ESP Visualization tool (ESP-VT)

The ESP-VT is an interactive knowledge platform that allows users to share information on ecosystem services maps, data and mapping methods.

ESP-VT is a joint initiative of the Ecosystem Service Partnership.s Working Groups on Mapping and Modelling ES, developed and supported by the Joint Research Centre of the European Commission (JRC-EC) and the Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (CSIRO) of Australia.
